# 2. Formal Definition of X-Space

    X = (XΔ, XΦ, XM, Xκ)

Its geometry is inherited from Flexion Space Theory.

    T_int emerges from the structural evolution of XM.

- XΔ depends on XΦ (energy enables differentiation).
